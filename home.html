World's Biggest University

Being established in 2002, Daffodil International University has become a prominent private
university in Bangladesh. Our 150 Acre campus is full of life and positive energy, fostering
education and innovation for more than 20000 students. Here, students are encouraged to ask
questions and think, debate and collaborate with professors to take ownership of their learning.

Programs

We are offering 38 undergraduate and graduate
programs under 26 departments. Our curriculum

Tuition Fees

We offer affordable tuition fees for students with
the option to pay them in multiple installments to
create convenience for students.

Scholarship

We make sure all our students get the help they
need to complete the degree. Scholarships are
available for students with excellent academic
results as well as for the students who are
struggling financially.
